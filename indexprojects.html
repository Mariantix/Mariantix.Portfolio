Projects

Through multiple classes, each specializing in different areas of design, I have completed a variety of projects.

When approaching each project, I tend to apply the same design thinking process:

  1. Empathize
  2. Define
  3. Ideate
  4. Prototype
  5. Test

Here are a few examples of projects I have completed within the last year:

Project Name Related Discipline Example Description
Logo Name Brand Graphic Design Utilizing my first name, I graphically restyled it to create a lineart brand logo.
Brand Collection Graphic Design By following an overall theme, this case being the three most common hot drinks, I designed a three piece mug collection with related colour palettes, icons and relevant photography.
Branding Styleguide Graphic Design Creating a ficticious company, Journey Brewing, I created the entire company branding styleguide meant to guide any marketing and branding amterial.
